Everything You Need to Know

Clear answers regarding authenticity guarantees, payments, shipping, and custom jewelry orders.

Every timepiece undergoes a comprehensive 30-point mechanical and physical inspection by certified master watchmakers. We inspect movement components, serial laser engravings, dial printing, hands, and case proportions. All items come backed by a lifetime authenticity money-back guarantee.

Once your watch is verified in person at our private showroom or via fully insured armored shipping, we execute an instant wire transfer or immediate cash payment on the exact same day before final handover.
